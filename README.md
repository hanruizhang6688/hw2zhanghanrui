# Business Workflow: Client Meeting Follow-up Generator

* **Workflow:** Automatically generate professional client follow-up emails based on brief meeting notes.
* **User:** Sales representative or account manager.
* **Input:** Messy meeting bullet points, client name, and specific follow-up actions.
* **Output:** A complete, friendly, and professional email containing a greeting, a summary of key points, clear next steps, and a standard signature block (hzhan305@jh.edu).
* **Value:** Saves time spent on repetitive outbound emails, ensures a consistent and professional tone, and prevents critical follow-up actions from being missed.
